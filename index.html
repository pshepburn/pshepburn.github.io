Peter Hepburn

Hey there! I'm an assistant professor of Sociology at Rutgers University-Newark and a research fellow at the Eviction Lab at Princeton University. I received my PhD from the joint program in Sociology and Demography at the University of California, Berkeley in 2018. This site is meant to provide some basic information about me and my research.
